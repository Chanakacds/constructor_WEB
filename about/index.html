About Us

Both directors, Lucky and Hala , come from pacific backgrounds. This connection lead them to both start their building journey together as labourers for a family member. They were able to learn about the trade before going their separate ways and gain national qualification. After years of growing their knowledge and experience with bigger building company’s, they came together and decided that they were ready for a new challenge. Creating and running their own building company. On July 2016 Auckland Super City Builders and Renovators was born. with high work demands shortly after we decided to employ three hardworking and passionate apprentices to help us achieve our goals. The workmanship we share on our jobs show our passion for the trade.
